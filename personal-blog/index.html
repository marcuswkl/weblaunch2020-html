The three stages of my life during the MCO period.

The first stage: Celebration

After the announcement of MCO, all lectures were moved to online.
No need to wake up early to drive to campus then come back home traffic jam.

The second stage: Boredom

After the initial celebration, I started to miss the campus life.
Everyday sit at home listen to online lectures very boring.

The third stage: Excitement

After MOHE announced campus reopened, I finally got to return to campus.
I got to meet my friends who have gained weight and grown long hair since the MCO.
